Welcome to the home of Dunfermline Reign Basketball

About Us

Dunfermline Reign Basketball Club (sc048978) are Fife's only National & Local League basketball team. Based at St Columba's RC HS - we are a charity that develops local people through basketball. We offer sessions that emphasise strong fundamentals, excellent coaching, player and individual development and transparent administration and communication. Whether you want to learn new skills, meet new people, compete or just have fun we have a session for you.

Our Vision

Dunfermline Reign Basketball Club’s vision is to develop people of all ages through basketball, male or female – playing or volunteering. We are proud to have been part of the life of so many people over the years and we use sport to develop skills that transcend the court into people’s lives.

Our Behaviours

Under 10 & 12 players Have Fun & Work Hard

As a Dunfermline Reign Player I commit to working hard and having fun in every session and game.

Under 14 players are also Willing to Learn & Resilient

When I progress to U14s I will also push myself outside my comfort zone, make mistakes, and be willing to take feedback on board. I will also fight through difficult times and use them to learn.

Under 16 players also Take Ownership & Give Back

When I progress to U16s I will take ownership over my own development both on and off the court, including appropriate planning of my time. I will also volunteer to give time back to help the club thrive.

Under 18 & Senior players are also Role Models

When I progress to U18 & Senior level I will have built a strong set of values and I commit to being a great role model for the club.
